Laptop Guide

Ram

  • Screen Size: 13.3" - 17.3"
  • Resolution: 1366x768 to 4K
  • Weight: 1.1kg - 2.5kg
  • Portable: Choose 13"-14"
  • Gaming/Work: Choose 15"-17"

Performance Specs

  • RAM: 4GB to 32GB (8GB min)
  • Storage: 256GB to 2TB
  • Processor: 1.8GHz to 4.5GHz
  • Higher GPU = Better graphics
  • SSD storage recommended

Laptop Categories

  • Ultrabook: Thin & light design
  • Gaming: High performance
  • Workstation: Professional work
  • 2-in-1: Tablet + Laptop
  • Notebook: General purpose

Brand Guide

  • Gaming: MSI, Razer, ASUS ROG
  • Business: Lenovo, Dell, HP
  • Premium: Apple, Microsoft
  • Budget: Acer, ASUS
  • Choose based on needs

Operating Systems

  • Windows: Most versatile
  • macOS: Apple ecosystem
  • Linux: Development work
  • Chrome OS: Web-based tasks
  • No OS: Custom installation

Quick Tips

  • Check all specifications
  • Consider future-proof specs
  • Balance price vs performance
  • Match specs to your usage
  • Verify GPU compatibility
